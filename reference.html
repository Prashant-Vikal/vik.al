Point of view

Design quality is an architecture problem before it is a taste problem. Good products and good teams come from good structure: clear responsibilities, sound decisions, and tooling that makes the right thing the easy thing. Increasingly that structure includes AI, which I design to compute and recommend so teams move faster, while people keep judgment and the final call.
